Chicago Parks and Bike Routes

This webpage provides the locations of the City's parks and bike routes.

The City of Chicago has over 600 parks to walk and play in, more than 200 miles of bike lanes, and many more sites to visit. These are more than a great way to stay active but are fun ways to explore Chicago's 78 neighborhoods!
